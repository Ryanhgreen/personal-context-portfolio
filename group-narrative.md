# Group Narrative

## Overview

This repository describes a group model rather than a single company. The group combines a family investment vehicle, a central operating company, software and services businesses, and a community-led AI initiative. The structure matters because ownership, operational capability, product strategy, and market execution sit in different places.

## The Logic of the Group

Green Box Ventures sits at the capital and family wealth layer. It is intended to accumulate long-term value, hold strategic shareholdings, receive management charges and dividends over time, and provide capacity for future investments.

TAC Operations is the central operating company. It runs finance, operations, back-office operations, HR, and innovation projects. It is also the home for CEO and CTO capability. Its purpose is to provide leverage, discipline, and specialist support across the group. It should materially influence quality and direction, but it should not directly control delivery execution or impose a single culture across the sub-organisations.

Caterlytix is the primary strategic growth engine. It serves contract caterers and hospitality companies by connecting fragmented systems, structuring operational data, and enabling reporting, applications, and AI-led solutions. It is the clearest route to larger-scale recurring revenue and international expansion.

Pebble is a schools-facing operating business with direct product relationships in education. Its product set includes TALI, an income management system, and TILL, an EPOS platform for secondary schools. Pebble has its own market logic, but its data can integrate with Caterlytix where cross-business value exists.

ArroCMS is an emerging SaaS platform focused on training and programme management. It is built around communications and AI, and is relevant across schools, charities, and businesses. It is moving from emerging proposition towards scale-up and should be managed with a sharper SaaS discipline over time.

AGENTSdotNE is a community initiative focused on AI capability, ideas, and applications in the North East. It strengthens regional profile, surfaces talent, and creates innovation adjacency. Its strategic value is broader than direct revenue.

## Relationship Model

The businesses should be treated as connected but not collapsed into one another.

- Green Box Ventures is the ownership and long-term capital layer.
- TAC Operations is the shared capability and influence layer.
- Caterlytix, Pebble, and ArroCMS are operating businesses with distinct markets and commercial models.
- AGENTSdotNE is a strategic ecosystem layer that supports profile, talent, and innovation.

## How An AI Should Use This Context

When reasoning about decisions, separate four questions:

1. Which entity owns the economic upside?
2. Which entity provides the operational capability?
3. Which entity serves the customer or user directly?
4. Which capabilities should be shared across the group, and which should remain local?

Good recommendations should respect those distinctions. Poor recommendations usually blur ownership, operating support, and delivery accountability into one layer.

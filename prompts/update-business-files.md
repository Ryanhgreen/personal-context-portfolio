## Business Sales Pitch Files

Each business folder contains a `sales-pitch.md` file.

These files are designed to help refine:
- brand positioning
- sales and marketing messages
- customer personas
- qualification questions
- objections, buying triggers, and commercial fit

They should be used when preparing:
- proposals
- sales decks
- discovery calls
- outreach messaging
- positioning and messaging work

Use the prompt in `/prompts/update-business-files.md`.

Business name:
{{BUSINESS_NAME}}

Repo URL:
{{REPO_URL}}

Existing files:
- {{PATH_TO_CONTEXT_MD}}
- {{PATH_TO_PROJECTS_MD}} (if applicable)
- {{PATH_TO_SALES_PITCH_MD}}

New source material:
- {{PASTE RAW NOTES OR CHAT EXTRACTS HERE}}

Please update the business `context.md`, `projects.md` if relevant, and `sales-pitch.md` by merging the new information with the existing files.
Use the existing repo files as the source of truth unless the new material is more precise or clearly more current.
Return all files in full.

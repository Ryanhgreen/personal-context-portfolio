# Decision Log

## Decision: Make Caterlytix the Primary Growth Engine

**Status:** Active  
**Rationale:** Caterlytix has the strongest route to scale because it sits on repeatable middleware, operational data management, reporting, application development, and AI-enabled workflows for fragmented hospitality environments. It can combine SaaS revenue with professional services in a way that accelerates adoption and deepens account value.  
**Implication for Decisions:** Growth capital, platform design, and strategic business development should generally bias towards Caterlytix unless another initiative presents a clearly superior return profile.

## Decision: Use TAC Operations as Shared Capability, Not a Delivery Control Layer

**Status:** Active  
**Rationale:** The group needs a strong centre for finance, operations, HR, back-office support, and innovation work. That centre should improve quality and speed, but it should not flatten the accountability, delivery ownership, or culture of the operating businesses.  
**Implication for Decisions:** Centralise specialist support and strategic leverage, but keep market-facing execution decisions close to each business where possible.

## Decision: Standardise at the Integration and Data Layer First

**Status:** Active  
**Rationale:** Across the group, especially within Caterlytix, fragmented source systems are the recurring problem. Standardising the data and integration layer creates leverage for reporting, applications, automation, and LLM-enabled services without forcing every client or business unit into the same front-end operating pattern.  
**Implication for Decisions:** Favour Trac Core and Trac ODM evolution that reduces fragmentation and improves tenancy, reuse, and portability.

## Decision: Keep Green Box Ventures as Long-Term Family Capital Infrastructure

**Status:** Active  
**Rationale:** Green Box Ventures is most useful as the family investment vehicle that holds shareholding exposure in TAC Operations and accumulates value over time through management charges, dividends, and future investment capacity.  
**Implication for Decisions:** Treat Green Box Ventures as a capital and ownership layer, not as the operating centre of the group.

## Decision: Keep Community and Commercial Work Distinct but Connected

**Status:** Active  
**Rationale:** AGENTSdotNE creates strategic value through profile, relationships, talent spotting, and idea flow. That value is real, but it should not be confused with the direct commercial logic of the software and operating businesses.  
**Implication for Decisions:** Use community activity to strengthen network and innovation opportunities, while preserving clear commercial disciplines in the operating businesses.

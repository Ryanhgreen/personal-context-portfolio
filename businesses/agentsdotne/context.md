# AGENTSdotNE

## Role in the Group

AGENTSdotNE is a community initiative focused on developing AI capability, ideas, and applications in the North East.

## Strategic Value

- Raises profile in the regional tech sector
- Supports talent spotting
- Creates innovation adjacency
- Builds relationships that may support future collaboration and opportunity

## Position in the Wider Portfolio

AGENTSdotNE is not primarily a conventional software business. Its value comes from ecosystem strength, regional visibility, idea generation, and network effects rather than from a standard SaaS revenue model.

## What An AI Should Keep In Mind

- Use AGENTSdotNE when thinking about profile, regional influence, partnerships, and idea flow.
- Do not confuse community value with the commercial operating logic of the product businesses.
- Recommendations should keep the initiative credible, practical, and rooted in regional impact.

# AGENTSdotNE

## Overview

AGENTSdotNE is a North East England AI community focused on practical capability, experimentation, and regional collaboration.

It brings together builders, professionals, businesses, and universities through free, open meetups and lightweight digital infrastructure.

## Purpose

- build practical AI capability in the region
- connect builders, businesses, and institutions
- surface ideas and collaboration opportunities
- strengthen the North East tech ecosystem
- create visibility, trust, and momentum around AI

## Strategic Role

AGENTSdotNE is a community and ecosystem initiative, not a conventional software business.

Its value comes from:

- network effects and relationships
- talent visibility and access
- idea generation and early-stage opportunities
- regional profile and credibility

It supports the wider group through partnerships, recruitment adjacency, and future venture origination.

## Operating Model (Current)

- free and open meetups
- central venue: Northern Design Centre
- involvement with universities
- one-page lightweight website
- focus on building a waiting list
- simple email capture at the top of the page
- more detailed form further down (includes role)
- project ideas collected separately via a secondary form
- no active projects yet
- launch target: early summer 2026
- future projects to sit under a shared GitHub organisation

## Current Phase

Validation.

The priority is to test demand, build engagement, and confirm usefulness to the regional tech and business community before expanding or formalising.

## Governance

May evolve into a community interest company, but not at this stage.

## Guidance

- keep it lightweight and credible
- prioritise usefulness over hype
- do not overbuild before demand is proven
- treat it as community-first, pre-project
- emphasise regional impact and practical AI use

# Green Box Ventures

## Role in the Group

Green Box Ventures is the family investment vehicle within the group structure. Its role is to hold long-term value, own strategic interests, and provide a capital base for future investment activity.

## Economic Logic

- Holds shareholding in TAC Operations
- Receives management charges and dividends over time
- Builds family wealth through disciplined long-term ownership
- Preserves capacity for future investments rather than operating as a day-to-day trading entity

## Strategic Use

Green Box Ventures should be treated as the capital and ownership layer of the group. It is not the operating centre and should not become the default home for delivery work, shared services, or product execution.

## What An AI Should Keep In Mind

- Recommendations involving ownership, dividends, capital allocation, or long-term value creation should consider Green Box Ventures first.
- Recommendations about operational management should usually sit elsewhere.
- Keep a clear distinction between economic ownership and operating responsibility.

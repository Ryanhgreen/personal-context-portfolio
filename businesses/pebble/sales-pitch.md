# Sales Pitch - Pebble

## Brand Positioning

Pebble provides practical, reliable software for schools, helping them manage income and catering transactions through Tali and Till.

## Who We Help

- schools
- school business leaders
- catering teams within schools
- education organisations needing simple, dependable operational systems

## Ideal Customer Profiles

- primary and secondary schools
- multi-academy trusts
- school business and finance teams
- schools needing stronger catering and income management processes

## Customer Personas

### School Business Manager
Needs practical systems that reduce admin and improve control.

### Finance Lead
Needs accurate income management and straightforward reporting.

### Catering Lead
Needs dependable till and operational support.

### Senior Leader
Needs confidence that systems work without creating disruption.

## Problems We Solve

- clunky or outdated school systems
- manual administration around income and transactions
- poor visibility of school-level operational data
- unreliable or awkward catering workflows
- systems that are harder to use than they should be

## Core Value Proposition

Pebble helps schools manage income and catering operations more effectively through straightforward, dependable software that fits the realities of education settings.

## Why We Win

- strong understanding of school environments
- practical systems designed for real operational needs
- focus on reliability and usability
- ability to connect into wider group capabilities, including Caterlytix

## Key Sales Messages

- We build software for how schools actually operate.
- Reliability and usability matter more than feature theatre.
- Tali and Till help schools reduce friction and improve operational control.
- Our systems can form part of a wider connected data ecosystem.

## Supporting Proof Points

- Tali is an income management system
- Till is an EPOS platform for secondary schools
- direct route to market into schools
- supports ecosystem value when integrated with Caterlytix

## Common Objections

- "We already have a system."
- "Changing school systems feels disruptive."
- "Budgets are tight."
- "We do not want something overly complex."

## Questions to Qualify a Prospect

- What systems are you currently using for income and transactions?
- Where is admin or friction highest today?
- How important is ease of use for your team?
- Are there reporting or operational gaps you want to close?
- Is this a school-level need, or part of a wider trust initiative?
- Are you open to better integration with other operational systems?

## Buying Triggers

- dissatisfaction with current provider
- operational pain around admin or catering workflows
- change in leadership or system strategy
- trust-level desire for more consistency
- opportunity to connect school systems into wider reporting infrastructure

## Cross-Sell or Group Synergies

- Pebble data can flow into Caterlytix for broader reporting and intelligence
- TAC Operations can support scaling and operational consistency
- education relationships may create openings for ArroCMS in training-led initiatives

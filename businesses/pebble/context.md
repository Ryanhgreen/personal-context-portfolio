# Pebble

## Role in the Group

Pebble is the direct-to-school business within the group. It serves the education market with operational products that solve real transaction and income-management problems in schools.

## Product Set

- TALI: income management system
- TILL: EPOS platform for secondary schools

## Strategic Position

Pebble should be treated as a focused market-facing business with its own commercial logic, customer relationships, and product responsibilities. It is not just a channel for another group platform.

## Relationship to the Wider Group

- Can benefit from shared operating support through TAC Operations
- Can integrate data with Caterlytix where that creates meaningful value
- Provides another practical environment for testing interoperable data and workflow ideas

## What An AI Should Keep In Mind

- Recommendations should respect the needs of the schools market rather than forcing a hospitality model onto it.
- Integration with Caterlytix is a strategic option, not the sole purpose of the business.
- Product clarity and customer fit matter more than group neatness.

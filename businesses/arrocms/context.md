# ArroCMS

## Role in the Group

ArroCMS is a SaaS training and programme management platform built around communications and AI. It is designed to help schools, charities, and businesses launch and run learning or programme-led activity more effectively.

## Commercial Model

- SaaS licensing
- Programme commission
- Services commission

## Strategic Position

ArroCMS is an emerging business moving towards scale-up. It has a broader cross-sector market than some of the other businesses, but it still needs disciplined positioning, product clarity, and repeatable delivery patterns to scale well.

## Differentiation

- Strong communications orientation
- AI-enabled capability embedded in programme operations
- Applicability across education, charity, and business contexts

## What An AI Should Keep In Mind

- Treat ArroCMS as a product business that needs sharper scale-up logic, not as an internal side project.
- Recommendations should strengthen repeatability, product clarity, and SaaS quality.
- Cross-sector opportunity is an advantage only if the core proposition remains clear.

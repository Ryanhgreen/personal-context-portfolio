# Sales Pitch - ArroCMS

## Brand Positioning

ArroCMS is a SaaS platform that helps organisations create, deliver, and scale programmes that drive real implementation, using communications, structure, and AI to close the gap between training and action.

## Who We Help

- schools
- charities
- businesses
- programme creators
- organisations delivering structured change, learning, or engagement initiatives

## Ideal Customer Profiles

- organisations running programmes that require behaviour change, not just content delivery
- teams that need structured communications alongside learning
- programme creators looking for a scalable delivery platform
- organisations wanting a mix of SaaS, marketplace access, and service support

## Customer Personas

### Programme Owner
Needs a better way to deliver and scale a programme.

### Learning or Development Lead
Needs learning to translate into actual implementation.

### Charity or Education Leader
Needs a practical platform to drive adoption and outcomes.

### External Programme Creator
Needs a route to market and scalable delivery infrastructure.

## Problems We Solve

- programmes that look good on paper but fail in practice
- weak follow-through after training or onboarding
- fragmented delivery across content, communications, and implementation
- limited scale for programme creators
- poor engagement over time

## Core Value Proposition

ArroCMS helps organisations and programme creators deliver learning and change programmes that actually land, by combining structured programme delivery, communications, and AI-enabled support.

## Why We Win

- focuses on implementation rather than just training
- combines communication with structured delivery
- supports both programme owners and programme creators
- creates a scalable SaaS and marketplace model
- can support behaviour change alongside the wider group’s systems and data capabilities

## Key Sales Messages

- Training only matters if it changes behaviour.
- ArroCMS helps programmes cross the implementation gap.
- We combine learning, communications, and AI so delivery is more than content alone.
- The platform can support both direct delivery and marketplace-led growth.

## Supporting Proof Points

- SaaS platform built around communications and AI
- supports schools, charities, and businesses
- revenue from licensing, programme commission, and services commission
- positioned as an emerging scale-up opportunity within the group

## Common Objections

- "We already have an LMS."
- "This sounds like another training platform."
- "Will users actually engage?"
- "How is this different from content delivery tools?"

## Questions to Qualify a Prospect

- Are you trying to deliver content, or drive real implementation and behaviour change?
- What happens after someone completes the learning today?
- How important are communications and follow-through in your programme?
- Do you need to scale a programme across multiple users or organisations?
- Would a marketplace or partner-led model add value?
- Is the bigger problem learning delivery, engagement, or implementation?

## Buying Triggers

- poor results from existing learning or programme tools
- need to scale programmes without scaling delivery effort at the same rate
- desire to improve engagement and follow-through
- opportunity to commercialise third-party programmes
- need for a more structured implementation model

- can complement Caterlytix by supporting behaviour change or rollout around operational improvement
- may align with Pebble relationships where schools need programme delivery
- benefits from AGENTSdotNE ideas and AI experimentation
- can be supported operationally through TAC Operations

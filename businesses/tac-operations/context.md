# TAC Operations

## Role in the Group

TAC Operations is the central operating company for the group. It provides shared capability, operating discipline, and specialist leverage across the portfolio.

## Core Responsibilities

- Finance
- Operations
- Back-office operations
- HR
- Innovation projects
- Shared CEO and CTO capability

## Strategic Position

TAC Operations should shape standards, improve execution quality, and provide experienced support where the operating businesses would otherwise duplicate effort or lack specialist depth. Its influence should be strong, but its role is not to micromanage each business or impose one culture across the group.

## Boundaries

- Does not directly control delivery in sub-organisations
- Does not own the local culture of each operating business
- Should avoid becoming a bottleneck or a command layer

## What An AI Should Keep In Mind

- TAC Operations is usually the right lens for shared services, operating design, governance, and innovation capacity.
- Do not assume central influence means direct line control.
- Recommendations should preserve business accountability while using TAC capability where it creates leverage.

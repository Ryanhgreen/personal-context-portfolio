# Sales Pitch - Caterlytix

## Brand Positioning

Caterlytix helps contract caterers and hospitality organisations connect fragmented systems, standardise data, and unlock operational intelligence through Trac Core and Trac ODM.

## Who We Help

- contract caterers
- hospitality groups
- multi-site foodservice operators
- enterprise organisations with fragmented operational systems

## Ideal Customer Profiles

- organisations with multiple disconnected systems across EPOS, CRM, payments, product, finance, or operational platforms
- businesses that need better reporting, visibility, and decision-making
- operators seeking a scalable data layer without replacing every existing system
- large groups, including international operators, looking for a long-term partner

## Customer Personas

### Chief Operating Officer
Needs better operational visibility and consistency.

### Finance Director
Needs cleaner data, stronger reporting, and better control.

### Commercial Director
Needs insight into performance, margin, and growth opportunities.

### Digital or Technology Lead
Needs systems to integrate without creating more complexity.

### Managing Director or CEO
Needs a strategic partner that creates long-term value and stickiness.

## Problems We Solve

- fragmented systems that do not talk to each other
- inconsistent and unreliable reporting
- slow decision-making caused by poor data visibility
- over-reliance on manual workarounds
- difficulty scaling insight across large and complex estates

## Core Value Proposition

We standardise the integration and data layer through Trac Core, then give clients their own tenancy in Trac ODM so they can build reports, tools, and AI-enabled applications that create real commercial and operational value.

## Why We Win

- deep understanding of catering and hospitality operations
- ability to connect disparate and legacy systems
- standardisation at the bottom, flexibility at the top
- combines recurring SaaS with high-value professional services
- creates long-term organisational stickiness through embedded infrastructure

## Key Sales Messages

- We help you keep your systems and make them work together.
- We standardise the data layer so you can customise everything above it.
- We turn fragmented operational data into usable reporting, applications, and insight.
- We help you create value without forcing wholesale system replacement.

## Supporting Proof Points

- Trac Core provides middleware and integration standardisation
- Trac ODM provides dedicated client tenancy for reporting and data access
- supports LLM-enabled reports and applications
- revenue model combines SaaS with bespoke professional services
- designed for growth into enterprise and international markets

## Common Objections

- "We already have reporting tools."
- "Integration projects are always messy."
- "This sounds expensive or complex."
- "We do not want to replace our current systems."
- "How do we know this will scale across our estate?"

## Questions to Qualify a Prospect

- How many core systems are currently involved in your operation?
- Where are your biggest reporting or visibility gaps?
- How much manual effort goes into joining data today?
- Are you trying to replace systems, or make existing ones work together better?
- Do you need a standard data layer that can support future reporting and applications?
- How important is client-owned IP or capitalisable value in your decision-making?
- Who owns this problem internally: operations, finance, digital, or leadership?

## Buying Triggers

- growth creating more system complexity
- enterprise groups needing standardised reporting
- frustration with disconnected data and manual workarounds
- desire to build custom applications or AI-enabled tools on reliable data
- strategic pressure to improve insight without replacing core systems
